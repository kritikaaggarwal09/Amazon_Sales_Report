# 🏪 Amazon Sales Dashboard – Power BI Project

## 📌 Overview
This project present an interactive Power BI dashboard built using Amazon Sales Data. The dashboard is designed to provide meaningful business insights through data visualization and analytics. It helps users understand sales performance, customer behavior, product trends, and overall business growth.

The report contains two interactive tabs, each focused on different aspects of the sales analysis.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 1. 📈Sales Overview Dashboard
This page provides overview of Amazon sales insights including:

- Total Sales
- Year-To-Date(YTD) Sales
- Quater-To-Date(QTD) Sales
- YTD Product Sold
- Yearly Sales Analysis
- Monthly Sales Analysis
- Weekly Sales Analysis
- Product Wise Sales
- Top 5 Products by YTD Sales
- Average Product Price
- Total Sales by each Product

### 2. 📅 Product Analysis Dashboard
This tab focuses on product performance and detailed sales insights using interactive visuals and filters.

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

-The business generated a total sales revenue of **$8.42M** during the analysis period. 

-The Year-to-Date (YTD) sales reached **$2.18M**, while the Quarter-to-Date (QTD) sales stood at **$811.09K**.

-A total of **28K units** were sold YTD, reflecting strong product demand across key categories.


## 📈 Sales Trend Analysis:-

-The company achieved its highest annual sales in 2020 within the period from 2019 to 2022, indicating exceptional market performance during that year. 🏆

-Quarter 4 (Q4) recorded the highest sales contribution, highlighting strong seasonal demand and year-end purchasing behavior. 📅

-On a monthly basis, December generated the highest sales, likely driven by holiday shopping trends and promotional campaigns. 🎄💰

-In contrast, February recorded the lowest sales performance, indicating a seasonal slowdown after peak holiday demand. 📉


## 🛍️ Product Performance Insights

-Among the Top 5 products, the highest YTD sales were generated by Nikon Wide Angle AF, making it the leading revenue-driving product. 📸

-Product category analysis shows that Cameras and Men’s Shoes contributed the highest sales revenue, demonstrating strong customer demand and market preference in these segments. 👟📷

-The Audio Video category generated the lowest sales, indicating weaker market performance and lower customer engagement compared to other categories. 🎧⚠️

---

## 🚀 Recommendations for Future Growth

1️⃣ Strengthen High-Performing Categories:-

-Increase inventory availability and marketing investment for Cameras and Men’s Shoes to maximize revenue growth. 📦

-Expand premium and complementary product offerings around the Nikon Wide Angle AF product line to boost cross-selling opportunities. 🎯

2️⃣ Capitalize on Seasonal Demand:--

-Since Q4 and December show peak sales performance, launch early promotional campaigns and targeted holiday marketing strategies. 🎉

Introduce:

-Festive discounts 🎁

-Product bundles 🛒

-Loyalty rewards ⭐

-Limited-time offers ⏳

3️⃣ Improve Low-Performing Periods:--

To address lower sales in February, implement:-

-Flash sales ⚡

-Customer retention campaigns 📧

-Personalized promotions 🎯

-End-of-season clearance events 🏷️

4️⃣ Optimize Underperforming Categories:--

Conduct detailed analysis of the Audio Video category to identify gaps in:

-Pricing strategy

-Customer preferences

-Marketing effectiveness 🔎

-Consider repositioning products or discontinuing consistently low-performing items. 📊

5️⃣ Enable Data-Driven Decision Making:--

Develop predictive sales forecasting models to improve inventory planning and demand forecasting. 🤖

-Monitor customer purchasing behavior regularly to enhance personalized recommendations and improve conversion rates. 📈
